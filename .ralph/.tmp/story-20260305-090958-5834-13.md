### US-013: Align naming and formatting conventions across modules
Status: in_progress
Depends on: US-010

Description:
As a maintainer, I want consistent naming and code style conventions so that the refactored codebase is easier to navigate and review.

Acceptance Criteria:
- [ ] Apply a documented naming convention for files, symbols, and module responsibilities across all touched paths.
- [ ] Ensure lint/format outputs are clean and predictable while preserving behavior.
- [ ] Example: similarly scoped services follow the same naming structure and export style conventions.
- [ ] Negative case: ambiguous or duplicated naming that obscures behavior is rejected during review and corrected before merge.
- [ ] Update refactor guide notes for future contributors with examples of accepted conventions.
